Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

kape.stimmie.dev lets you make voluntary donations to support open source projects. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

What we collect

  • Payment details — card or e-wallet information you enter at checkout. Payments are processed by PayMongo; we do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Optional donor message — if you choose to leave a short note with your donation, we store it to display on the site (unless you ask us to remove it).
  • Technical logs — basic server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) for security and debugging.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not use your information for third-party advertising.
  • We do not require an account to donate.

How we use donations data

Payment confirmation, receipt fulfillment, optional public thank-you messages, and aggregate reporting (e.g. total raised). PayMongo handles PCI-compliant payment processing under their own privacy policy.

Data retention

Donation records are kept for accounting and tax purposes as required. Optional messages can be removed on request.
